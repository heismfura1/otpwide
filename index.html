About Us

OTP WIDE is a Rwandan clothing brand founded in 2024 by students, merging Christianity with contemporary culture. The brand designs apparel that reflects both spiritual beliefs and cultural identity, using Christian symbols and modern aesthetics. Committed to ethical fashion, OTP WIDE supports local artisans and sustainable practices. Drawing inspiration from Rwanda's rich heritage, the brand incorporates traditional patterns, allowing wearers to connect with their roots.
